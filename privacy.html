Privacy

Clear by design.

Last updated
July 4, 2026

Verisib is designed to collect as little information as possible while giving you a clear way to start a conversation.

01

Information you choose to send

The contact form does not submit information to or store information on this website. When you use it, your device’s email application opens with a prepared message. You decide whether to send it.

If you email Verisib, we receive the information included in your message and use it to respond to your inquiry.

02

Keep sensitive information out of email

Please do not include medical records, Social Security numbers, financial account details, passwords, or other highly sensitive information in an initial message. General information about the situation is enough to begin.

03

Website data

This static website does not use advertising cookies or third-party analytics. Our hosting provider may process limited technical information, such as IP address and request details, to deliver and protect the site according to its own policies.

04

Questions

Questions about this notice can be sent by selecting Email Us.
