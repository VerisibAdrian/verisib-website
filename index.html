Questions, answered

Clarity before the first conversation.

Every situation is different. These answers are a useful place to start.

When should we contact Verisib?

Reach out whenever senior living becomes part of the conversation—whether you are planning ahead, facing a change in care needs, or coordinating a time-sensitive transition.

What types of senior living can you help us explore?

We help families explore independent living, assisted living, memory care, and other options appropriate to the situation and available in the desired area.

Can a care professional refer a patient or client?

Yes. Hospital, rehabilitation, case management, and other care professionals can contact Verisib to begin a placement conversation with the individual or family.

What information is helpful at the beginning?

A general sense of care needs, preferred location, timing, budget, and what matters most to the person is enough to begin. Please do not send sensitive medical or financial records through the website.
