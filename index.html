Care options

Support for the care your loved one needs.

Not sure which level of care is right? That is a normal place to begin. We will talk it through with you.

Assisted Living

For older adults who want independence while receiving help with daily needs such as meals, medication reminders, bathing, dressing, or mobility.

Memory Care

For people living with Alzheimer’s or other forms of dementia who benefit from a secure setting, consistent routines, and specialized support.

About Verisib

Local guidance with a personal touch.

Verisib is a Phoenix-area senior placement service created to help families make an important care decision with less stress and more clarity. We take time to understand your loved one, explain the options in plain language, and help you find a community that feels right.

Our guidance is free to families. If you choose a participating community, that community may pay Verisib a referral fee.

Common questions

A few helpful answers.

Is Verisib really free for families?

Yes. Our placement guidance is provided at no cost to your family. If you choose a participating community, that community may pay Verisib a referral fee.

When should I call Verisib?

Call whenever assisted living or memory care becomes part of the conversation—whether you are planning ahead or need help soon.

What should I know before the first call?

You do not need to have everything figured out. A general sense of care needs, preferred location, timing, and budget is enough to begin.
