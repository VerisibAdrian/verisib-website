Care options

Support for the care your loved one needs.

Not sure which level of care is right? That is a normal place to begin. We will talk it through with you.

Assisted Living

For older adults who want independence while receiving help with daily needs such as meals, medication reminders, bathing, dressing, or mobility.

Memory Care

For people living with Alzheimer’s or other forms of dementia who benefit from a secure setting, consistent routines, and specialized support.

About Verisib

Built to help families make the right decision.

Verisib is not a traditional placement agency. It is a trusted local resource created to give families clear, compassionate guidance from someone who understands assisted living firsthand.

If SunnySide Assisted Living is the best fit, Adrian will recommend it. If another trusted home is better because of location, care needs, or budget, he will honestly recommend that option instead.

Our guidance is free to families and always centered on what is right for your loved one.

Common questions

A few helpful answers.

Is Verisib really free for families?

Yes. Adrian's guidance is provided at no cost to your family, with no pressure to choose a particular home.

Will Verisib always recommend SunnySide?

No. If SunnySide is the best fit, Adrian will recommend it. If another trusted home better fits your location, care needs, or budget, he will recommend that option honestly.

When should I call Verisib?

Call whenever you have questions—even if you are unsure where to start or whether assisted living is the right next step.

What should I know before the first call?

You do not need to have everything figured out. A general sense of care needs, preferred location, timing, and budget is enough to begin.
